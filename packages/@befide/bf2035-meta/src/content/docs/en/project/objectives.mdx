## Impact on donors

- Satisfaction after repeated request
- Communicate economic importance and economic potential
- Communicate the importance of the test facilities and their financing
- Restore openness towards ERL technology

## Impact on community

- Independent improvement of the situation
  - through involvement of the entire community
  - Approximate completeness
- Strengthening the sense of community

## Correctness/appropriateness

- Separation of interdisciplinary and specialised strategy
- Data-based
- Traceability
