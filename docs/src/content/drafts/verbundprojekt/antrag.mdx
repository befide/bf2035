## Goals

- Professionalising community management through the creation of a formal association.
- Community building through demonstration of excellemnce and opportunities for collaboration.
  - Rebranding accelerator physics as accelerator and beam science.
  - Implementing a community-curated transdisciplinary ontology creation process (including physical and engineering aspects, cavity-based and plasma-based accelerator technologies, including laser and plasma physics).
  - Use the ontology as a basis for
    - Implementation of a bibliometric-based excellence estimation process
    - Implementation of a community curated inventory process.
    - Implementation of a community curated research agenda process.
    - TLR monitoring of accelerating techniques.
    - Inter-university collaboration in teaching (common curriculum, common content pool, common teaching events).
